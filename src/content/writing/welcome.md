For five seasons now we have been reading slowly and closely — Hegel, Deleuze, Heidegger, Derrida, Nietzsche — less to master these texts than to think alongside them. The podcast was always meant as an invitation rather than a lecture: a way of showing what thinking *with* a text actually looks like, hesitations and wrong turns included.

This site is the next part of that experiment. It gives us somewhere to do three things we couldn't do in an audio feed alone.

**A home for the seasons.** Every episode, gathered by season and by thinker, with the cover art that has come to mark each one — the Hegel Tapes, the Deleuze Cast, the Heidegger Files, Truth or Derrida, the Season of the Nietzsche.

**A place to write.** Some thoughts want to be read rather than heard. We'll publish essays, provocations, and reading notes here — including pieces from our ongoing work on a rhetorical theory of language, and the materials for our Critique reading group.

**A way to stay in touch.** A short email list, an inbox, and (soon) somewhere to keep the conversation going.

Season Six — *Being and Time* — is in production now. Until then, start anywhere. The point was never to begin at the beginning.
